    Soccer

    Adult Soccer Leagues

    Are the fees per team or per individual?
    Per team, but most teams will divide it evenly between each player rostered.
    How much does a session cost?
    The cost varies based on the number weeks in a given session. Please contact us for current league pricing.
    How do I pay my team fee?
    You may pay online or over the phone or in-person at the guest services at the center during operating hours.
    How long is a session?
    Most sessions are 10 weeks, but sometimes they are shorter due to holidays, etc.
    How many people can be on a roster?
    14 max
    What is the minimum age?
    16 years old
    Can someone be added to a roster after the session starts?
    Yes, but rosters lock after the third game.
    What leagues have the intimidation rule?
    Women's C, Coed C, and Coed D
    Is a membership required to play?
    Yes
    Is a signed annual waiver required to play?
    Yes
    Do you have to wear shin guards?
    Yes! As described in our facility rules, shin guards are absolutely required and strictly enforced by our referees.
    What type of shoes are allowed on the field?
    Indoor, turf, or tennis shoes and socks are required. Cleats are strictly prohibited as described in our soccer league rules.
    What is the subbing policy?
    If you are rostered on a team for the day you are subbing, you get 1 free sub. If you sub again that day, the cost is $10 per game. If you are not rostered on any team playing that day, the cost is $10 per game. In all cases, before playing, you must obtain a "sub slip" from guest services. Any costs must be must be paid to guest services before playing. Please refer to our Substitute Players (Subs) policy in our league rules for further details.

    Kickin' Krakens

    How many weeks is the session?
    8
    How many days a week?
    Once a week.
    Are there games?
    No, only practice.
    Are parents required to be on the field?
    The only parent-assisted class is the 1.5-2 year old class.
    What attire is recommended?
    A Kickin' Krakens shirt is provided. We recommend athletic wear that is easy to move around in. Some athletic wear is available for sale at our guest services desk
    Are shin guards required?
    No, but we do recommend them. It will help kids get comfortable wearing shin guards with soccer socks over them. Some shin guards and socks are available for sale at our guest services desk.
    What shoes are appropriate?
    Indoor turf shoes or tennis shoes (no outdoor soccer cleats).
