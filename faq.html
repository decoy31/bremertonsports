    Soccer

    Adult Soccer Leagues

    Are the fees per team or per individual?
    Per team, but most teams will divide it evenly between each player rostered.
    How much does a session cost?
    The cost varies based on the number of weeks in a given session. Please contact us for current league pricing.
    How do I pay my team fee?
    You may pay online or over the phone or in-person at the Front Desk during center operating hours.
    How long is a session?
    Most sessions are 8 weeks, but sometimes they can be shorter due to holidays, etc.
    How many people can be on a roster?
    14 max
    What is the minimum age?
    18 years old. No exceptions.
    Can someone be added to a roster after the session starts?
    Yes, but rosters lock after the third week of games.
    What leagues have the intimidation rule?
    Women's C, Coed C, and Coed D
    Is a membership required to play?
    Yes
    Is a signed annual waiver required to play?
    Yes
    Do you have to wear shin guards?
    Yes! As described in our facility rules, shin guards are required.
    What type of shoes are allowed on the field?
    Indoor, turf, or tennis shoes and socks are required. Cleats are strictly prohibited as described in our soccer league rules.
    What is the subbing policy?
    • If you are rostered on a team for the day you are subbing, you get 1 free sub.
    • If you sub again that day, the cost is $12 per game.
    • If you are not rostered on any team playing that day, the cost is $15 per game.
    • If you are not rostered on any team playing that day, and do not want to pay for a membership, the cost is $20 per game.
    • In all cases, before playing, you must obtain a "sub slip" from Front Desk. Any costs must be paid to Front Desk before playing. Please refer to our Substitute Players (Subs) policy in our league rules for further details.
    What is the Free Agent List?
    We only coordinate the soccer leagues that play at the center; however, we do offer the ability to be put on a Free Agent List to try and be picked up by team managers, or if there are enough players we will create a team from those players (in our Adult Leagues Only). Our League Coordinator will “mentor” the team or a player on the team over the session they are playing to show them how someone manages a team, then it will be up to that group if they want to stick together for the next session.

    Youth Soccer Leagues

    Are the fees per team or per individual?
    Per individual. Please review our pricing on our Youth Leagues page for current pricing.
    How much does a session cost?
    The cost varies based on the number weeks in a given session.
    How do I pay my individual fee?
    You may pay online or over the phone or in-person at the Front Desk at the center during operating hours.
    How long is a session?
    Most sessions are 8 or 9 weeks, but sometimes they are shorter or longer due to holidays, etc.
    How many people can be on a roster?
    • Big Field Teams: Minimum of 10 players per team (Max 14 players) + up to 2 Coaches
    • Small Field Teams: Minimum of 8 players + up to 2 Coaches
    What is the minimum age?
    4 years old. No exceptions.
    Can someone be added to a roster after the session starts?
    Yes, but rosters lock after the third game.
    Is a membership required to play or coach?
    It is included in the player fees for the session for players. See Youth Leagues page for player pricing. Coaches do need a membership to be down in the player bench areas.
    Is a signed annual waiver required to play or coach?
    Yes.
    Do players need a birth certificate?
    Yes, we require a copy of birth certificates for proof of age.
    Do players have to wear shin guards?
    Yes! As described in our facility rules, shin guards are absolutely required and strictly enforced by our referees. No exceptions.
    What type of shoes are allowed on the field?
    Indoor, turf, or tennis shoes and socks are required. Cleats are strictly prohibited as described in our soccer league rules.
    What is the subbing policy?
    • If you are rostered on a team for the day you are subbing, you get 1 free sub.
    • If you sub again that day, the cost is $12 per game.
    • If you are not rostered on any team playing that day, the cost is $15 per game.
    • If you are not rostered on any team playing that day, and do not want to pay for a membership, the cost is $20 per game.
    • In all cases, before playing, you must obtain a "sub slip" from Front Desk.
    • Any costs must be paid to Front Desk before playing. Please refer to our Substitute Players (Subs) policy in our league rules for further details.
    Why does BSC not have house teams?
    BSC only coordinates the soccer leagues that play at the center. BSC does not provide coaching.
    Is there a free agent list for the youth leagues?
    Yes. We have a free agent list for players looking to join a team. You can be added to this list by requesting it directly from us, or by signing into your account online. To add your child to the “LOOKING FOR TEAM” list online, please sign into your account, click on “Register,” and then locate your child's age group. You will see “LOOKING FOR TEAM” as an available option there.
