    Preschool Prodigies

    The Prodigies curriculum uses coloring books and interactive video lessons to teach music concepts to young children. In the process, children will develop their listening and motor skills through music. Children can also create music by playing along on desk bells, boom whackers, xylophones, or even an app. This class is designed for children 3-6, though some kids 3 years old may need their parents' help to play along with the videos in the lessons. Parent participation is encouraged and welcomed.

    Classes run: September 8th, 2025 to May 25th, 2026
