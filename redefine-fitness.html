    Bootcamps

    Redefine Fitness Bootcamps at Bremerton Sports Center are approximately 60-minute long, functional fitness classes. Get your body moving and challenge yourself physically in a safe and supportive environment. Whether you have taken a long period of time away from fitness training or are an avid fitness enthusiast, these classes will have something to offer you!

    About Lindsey

    My name is Lindsey Dahl and I am the Owner and Instructor for Redefine Fitness. I am a Certified Personal Trainer and Registered Nurse. I enjoy spending time with my two sons and husband, playing soccer, and painting. I have lived in Kitsap County my entire life and have enjoyed the soccer center since I was in high school. I look forward to building new relationships within the community and helping Bremerton Sports Center continue to grow.
