    Register for Cornhole

    Open drop-in (Tuesdays)

    Open drop-in on Tuesdays is for individuals as the group plays switcholio for the first 2 hours with an elimination tournament at the end.

    Open doubles/couples (Thursdays)

    Open doubles/couples on Thursdays is intended for those that want to pick their own partner and play with a specific partner. Teams will play a round robin before being separated into two brackets for elimination play.
