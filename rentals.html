    Rentals

    The three fields, two party rooms, and score boards of the facility are available to rent individually. Please note:

    • Annual membership is required: $35 / renter
    • Peak season: Sat-Sun all day from September 1st through May 31st
    • Peak times: M-F 6:00pm - 10:00pm all year

    For rentals more than 1 week in advance or wanting multiple bookings, please email rentals@bremertonsports.com.

    Field Rentals

    There are two large fields and one small field available to rent.

      Large Field 1/2 Large Field Small Field
    Peak Season/Time (youth) $100 $50 $75
    Non-Peak Season/Time (youth) $80 $40 $60
    Peak Season/Time (adult) $120 $60 $90
    Non-Peak Season/Time (adult) $100 $50 $75

    Score board rental: $25 + $25 deposit / score board
