    Athletic Development Program

    Join Academy Performance Enhancement's (APE) 6-week athletic development program at Bremerton Sports Center to build your athletes confidence. Every session includes injury prevention training, daily mentoring, as well as training in speed, agility, mobility, and strength conditioning. Open to all athletes 8-17yo. Your advanced training opportunity will be capped off with free admission into the Academy Combine, a separate community event which will give your athlete the opportunity to display their personal best across a range of athletic skills events. Learn more at TheAcademyApe.com.

    About Coach Jay

    Coach Jay is an experienced Performance Enhancement Specialist and Certified Personal Trainer serving athletes of all ages in Kitsap county. His passion for mentoring young athletes stems from his years as a basketball and football Coach. He began his journey as a personal trainer with his own son, in their home Gym. He quickly noticed the benefit of training his own son and wanted to make the same opportunity available to all athletes in the Kitsap area. He now helps develop not only football and basketball players but athletes of all sports who are in need of advanced sports performance training. His training philosophy is rooted in developing focused and disciplined athletes, while preventing injury and unleashing their full potential. Learn about personal training and other APE athletic opportunities at TheAcademyApe.com.
