    Bremerton Sports Center offers youth indoor soccer leagues for kids U5 to U18 and all skill levels. Teams with premier level players must register a minimum of one year up. Games range from 8am Saturday morning until 9pm Saturday night. The exception is High School Coed where games will be scheduled for either late Saturday or Sunday afternoon.

    If you have a player that does not have a team to play on, please email us at info@bremertonsports.com with their name (first and last), gender, birth date, and your contact information.

    Current league starts: November 18th, 2023
    Current league ends: January 27th, 2023
    Team sign ups reopen: November 20th, 2023
    No games: November 25th, 2023 and December 23rd, 2023
    Next league starts: February 3rd, 2024
    Next league ends: March 30th, 2024

    For schedule requests, questions, concerns, or feedback related to youth soccer leagues, please email: info@bremertonsports.com.

    Pricing

    All team fees must be paid in full in order to start the team's first game.

    • Annual membership: $25 / youth player / year
    • Coed 4, 5, 6, 7 Leagues: $720 / 9 game session
    • Girls 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15 Leagues: $1080 / 9 game session
    • Boys 8, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15 Leagues: $1080 / 9 game session
    • High School Coed 14, 15, 16, 17, 18 Leagues: $1080 / 9 game session

    Coed 4, 5, 6, 7 Leagues

    • 4 v 4; no keepers
    • Roster max: 8 players / team
    • Small field
    • Size 3 ball

    Girls 8, 9, 10, 11 / Boys 8, 9, 10, 11 Leagues

    • 6 v 6 + keepers
    • Roster max: 14 players + coaches / team
    • Players max: 12-13 players / team
    • Coach max: 1-2 coaches / team
    • Large field
    • Size 4 ball

    Girls 12, 13, 14, 15 / Boys 12, 13, 14, 15 Leagues

    • 5 v 5 + keepers
    • Roster max: 14 players + coaches / team
    • Players max: 12-13 players / team
    • Coach max: 1-2 coaches / team
    • Large field
    • Size 5 ball

    High School Coed 14, 15, 16, 17, 18 Leagues

    • 5 v 5 + keepers
    • Roster max: 14 players + coaches / team
    • Players max: 12-13 players / team
    • Coach max: 1-2 coaches / team
    • Minimum 2 female field players required at all times
    • Large field
    • Size 5 ball
