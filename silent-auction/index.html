    Silent Auction Fundraiser

    Bremerton Sports Center is hosting a silent auction fundraiser to raise money to replace the turf on Field #2 at the facility. The turf on Field #2 was installed when the first part of the facility was built in 2001. Since then, regular maintenance and patches have extended the life of the turf as much as we could, but turf only technically has a life span of 15 years. It is time for new turf and we are turning to our wonderful community for help in this large, $100,000 endeavor.

    Important dates and times are as follows:

    • Bidding opens: Monday, September 5th, 2022 @ 12PM
    • Bidding closes: Saturday, September 17th, 2022 @ 9:30PM
    • Physical/Digital item submission deadline: Saturday, September 17th, 2022 @ 5PM
    • Physical item donation drop-off deadline: Saturday, September 17th, 2022 @ 5PM
    • Silent auction item winners notified: Sunday, September 18th, 2022 by EOD

    For the most up-to-date information on the Silent Auction including announcements, please follow Bremerton Sports Center on Facebook.

    Donating

    There are a few ways you can help the center replace the turf:

    1. Donate Silent Auction item(s) by either:
      • Filling out this donate item form (requires a Google login).
      • Emailing: silent-auction@bremertonsports.com
    2. Bid on Silent Auction items.
    3. For cash donations, fill out and submit this cash donation form.

    We will be accepting silent auction donations all the way up until one hour before the start of the Grand Reopening. Cash donations will be accepted indefinitely.

    Recognition

    For those who donate items, cash, and/or win an item during the Silent Auction, Bremerton Sports Center would like to thank those folks by providing the option of adding their name to the wall to the right-hand side of the new mural on the mezzanine. This is completely optional and Bremerton Sports Center will confirm with those whose name we would like to put up on the wall before we do. Some customization of the text of the name will be afforded as well.

    For those who have donated, thank you! Bremerton Sports Center will be following up with you in the days that follow the Silent Auction's conclusion to confirm whether you would like your names up on the wall and what the name(s) should be.

    Other Important Information

    The information you submit in any of the forms related to the Silent Auction will only be used to coordinate Silent Auction logistics. Bremerton Sports Center will not use the information for any other purpose.

    Bremerton Sports Center is privately owned and operated by Bremerton Sports Center, LLC, a for-profit company. Therefore, donations made to Bremerton Sports Center will not be tax deductible. Please consult your financial or tax advisor if you have financial questions or concerns about donating.
